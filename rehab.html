Drug-Free Withdrawal from Drugs and Alcohol

Each individual is given around the clock supervision whilst on withdrawal until they reach the point where they are eating and sleeping well and are not experiencing any discomfort. Specific and natural nutrition is administered to assist the body to repair and rebuild itself. This can have additional benefits, such as helping the individual to sleep better.
On this step each person is regularly given a specially blended formula of certain vitamins. In addition, calcium and magnesium is given as this can sometimes aid with relaxation and muscle cramping.

How many pupils do you prefer to address in a session?

Does Narconon Drug Education work with other organisations?

Yes. We sit on the National Drug Prevention Alliance and work with other local groups wherever possible.

Is there any religious content in the Narconon Drug Education format?

No. Narconon Drug Education UK is a secular charity.
